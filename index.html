LECHE FLAN

Ingredients
  • 10 egg yolks
  • 2 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the eggs

  1. Prepare the eggs by separating the yolks from the whites.
  2. Only the yolks are needed.

Step 2: Make the caramel

  1. In a saucepan, melt the sugar over medium heat until it turns golden brown.
  2. Carefully pour the caramel into a flan mold, tilting to coat the bottom evenly. Set aside to cool and harden.

Step 3: Prepare the flan mixture

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the egg yolks, sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract.
  2. Whisk until smooth and well combined.

Step 4: Strain the flan mixture

  1. Strain the mixture through a wire sieve to remove any solids.

Step 5: Pour the mixture into the mold

  1. Pour the strained flan mixture into the caramel-coated mold.

Step 6: Bake the flan

  1. Place the flan mold in a larger baking dish and fill the dish with hot water halfway up the sides of the mold (water bath).
  2. Bake for about 50-60 minutes, or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.

*A lid was used in place of foil

Step 7: Cool and refrigerate

  1. Remove the flan from the oven and let it cool to room temperature.
  2. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

Step 8: Serve the flan

  1. Serve the flan warm or at room temperature.
